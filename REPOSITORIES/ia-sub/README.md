# Subsea Hub
Welcome to the **Subsea Hub** repository.  This repository serves as the central
aggregation point for all projects, libraries, tools, and documentation related
to the **Subsea** initiative within Petrobras. The Subsea initiative focuses on
applying Artificial Intelligence (AI) and advanced analytics to subsea
engineering, operations, and data management.

---

## About Subsea
**Subsea** is a Petrobras initiative dedicated to leveraging AI and digital
technologies to enhance subsea field development, operation, and maintenance.
The platform supports integration, collaboration, and innovation across multiple
teams and disciplines, aiming to optimize performance, safety, and reliability
in subsea environments.

---

## Repository Structure
This hub repository provides:
  - **Links to related repositories** (core libraries, modules, tools,
    documentation, etc.)
  - **Guidelines** for internal collaboration and contribution
  - **Overview** of the Subsea architecture and roadmap
  - **Resources** for onboarding and support
