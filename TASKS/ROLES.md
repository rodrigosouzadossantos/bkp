# Roles Description

## PO (Product Owner)
  The Product Owner is responsible for defining the product vision, managing the
product backlog, and ensuring that the development team delivers value to the
business. They prioritize features and work closely with stakeholders to gather
requirements and feedback.
  The Product Owner is the voice of the customer and ensures that the team is
building the right product.

## PM (Project Manager)
  The Project Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and closing
projects. They manage the project scope, schedule, and resources to ensure
successful project delivery. The Project Manager coordinates with the team,
stakeholders, and other departments to ensure that project goals are met on time
and within budget. They also identify and mitigate risks, resolve issues, and
communicate project status to stakeholders.

## LA (lead Argist)
  The Lead Argist is responsible for leading the development team and ensuring
that the technical aspects of the project are executed effectively. They provide
technical guidance, mentor team members, and ensure that the team follows best
practices and coding standards.
  The Lead Argist collaborates with the Product Owner and Project Manager to
ensure that the technical implementation aligns with the product vision and
project goals. They also play a key role in code reviews, architecture
decisions, and technical problem-solving.

## Artificial Intelligence (AI) Specialist
  The AI Specialists are responsible for designing, developing, and implementing
artificial intelligence solutions. They work closely with the Product Owner and
Lead Argist to understand the requirements and identify opportunities for AI
integration.
  The AI Specialist develops machine learning models, natural language
processing algorithms, and other AI technologies to enhance the product's
capabilities.  They also stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in AI and
ensure that the solutions they develop are ethical, scalable, and effective in
meeting the needs of the users and the business.
  Those specialists may also be involved in data analysis, feature engineering,
and model evaluation to ensure that the AI solutions are accurate and
performant. They must evaluate all scenarios around available data in order to
make informed decisions about the AI implementation and its impact on the
product accuracy and performance.
  Finally,they also collaborate with the team to integrate AI solutions into the
product and ensure that they align with the overall product vision and project
goals.
